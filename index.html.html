About Northmark M&A

Intelligence at the intersection of deals and capital.

Northmark M&A is a specialized advisory and research platform serving buyers, sellers, and investors across North American middle market and lower middle market transactions. We curate a directory of qualified advisors, maintain a record of closed transactions, and produce independent research — operating with the precision and discretion that complex deals demand.

Northmark M&A is anchored in principled, long-horizon thinking.

How we operate
◎
Discretion first

All deal flow is handled with strict confidentiality. We work under NDA before disclosing any principal-identifying information to any party on the platform.

⊖
Independent perspective

Northmark M&A does not take retainers from buyers or sellers on listed deals. Our research is never commissioned by deal parties — we remain structurally independent.

◷
Long-horizon thinking

We advise with a view to sustainable outcomes — for the business, its people, and its stakeholders.
